# SaaS Business Analytics: Customer Subscription & Revenue Insights Dashboard

## Project Overview
End-to-end data analytics project simulating the role of a Data Analyst at a
SaaS company — analyzing revenue, churn, customer value, and marketing
performance using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

## Business Problem
- How much revenue are we generating, and is it growing?
- Which subscription plans and customer segments are most valuable?
- Which customers are likely to churn, and why?
- Which marketing channels bring the best customers?
- How does support experience affect satisfaction and retention?

## Tools Used
Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib/seaborn), SQL, Power BI, Git/GitHub
